About Me

I am currently woking with Dr Amit Deshpande as a Research Fellow at Microsoft Research India on alignment and preference learning. I completed my B.Tech from IIT Madras (CSE) in July 2024, working in the Theory Group under Prof. Jayalal Sarma on complexity theory, more specifically Boolean function complexity. I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science and more recently, have been exploring problems in theoretical machine learning.
