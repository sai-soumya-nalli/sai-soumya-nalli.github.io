About Me

I am Sai Soumya Nalli, a Research Fellow at Microsoft Research India, working with Dr. Amit Deshpande on alignment and preference learning. I completed my B.Tech in Computer Science from IIT Madras, where I worked with Prof. Jayalal Sarma on complexity theory, particularly Boolean function complexity.

I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science and, more recently, in problems at the intersection of theory and machine learning.
